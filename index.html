Ada & Leo is a new kids' tech program focused on learning and creativity, led by veteran programmer and teacher Ben Wheeler.

The program is open to students age 6-9.

There will be an open house for students and parents on Saturday, Jan 30th, 9:30-10:15, where we will show the kinds of activities we do and answer questions.

The winter/spring 2016 session will be Saturdays from 9:30-10:45, Feb 6 through April 16 (10 classes, excluding Sat Feb 20, during February vacation.)

The cost will be $300 and will cover materials.

Please note the class is currently at capacity, but please join the waitlist and let us know about your schedule availability.

Topics will include programming, circuitry, 3d printing and design, robotics, strategic thinking, invention and design thinking. We will use creative and effective software and hardware tools and will play games that stimulate logic thinking.

Location: 483 Putnam Ave, between Throop and Tompkins Aves in Bed-Stuy, Brooklyn.

All teachers will be trained in first aid and have passed background checks. The program will be safety-conscious.

Even more importantly, we promise to care for the heart, mind and spirit of curiosity in your child as if they were our own children... (who will be in the class too!)
